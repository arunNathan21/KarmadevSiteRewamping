Clean Clan

Waste to Resource

Clean Clan website is the largest digital waste marketplace in India and connects suitable business partners using modern digital technology and machine learning. It only takes a few minutes to add or request a product and registration is completely free and online. Clean Clan's main mission is to introduce the principles of circular economics into the waste management of companies across all industries.

Our Services for Clean Clan

Clean Clan is a website we created for a customer that wanted to incorporate the ideas of circular economy into the waste management of businesses of all sizes.

We supply the client with services at reasonable prices that include testing and maintenance. We not only create websites, but we also offer marketing services such as SEO.

Our Strategies for Exquisite

The business analyst works with the customer to determine their genuine demands, in addition to providing the client with clear services from our firm.

As a result, it advances to the next step, a conversation with the project Manager. The designer creates a design after receiving the project manager's detailed model. With the client's approval, the development moves on to the next level.

We provide SEO services to the website once all development and testing is accomplished, which increases organic traffic.
