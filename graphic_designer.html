Graphic Designer

Graphic Designer

Full-time Intern

Job Description

We're on the lookout for a talented and driven Graphic Design Intern to join our dynamic team. As a Graphic Design Intern, you will have the exciting opportunity to contribute to our visual identity and brand presence. Your role will involve creating compelling and visually appealing graphics for various purposes, including digital marketing posts, personal branding, and various carousel posts for various sectors.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the marketing and creative teams to brainstorm ideas and concepts for graphics and visual content.
  • Create eye-catching designs for social media posts, website banners, email campaigns, advertisements, and other marketing materials.
  • Use graphic design software and tools to produce high-quality visuals that resonate with our target audience.
  • Ensure consistency and adherence to brand guidelines across all design projects.
  • Stay updated on design trends, techniques, and best practices to continuously improve the quality and effectiveness of our visual assets.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in graphic design software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator)
  • A strong portfolio showcasing creative and visually appealing design work.
  • Understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and layout techniques.
  • Ability to take direction and incorporate feedback to refine designs.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with team members.
  • Detail-oriented with a keen eye for aesthetics and visual storytelling.
